Spotify

Lyrics, metadata, and DJ from your Spotify account.

Install in Resonance
Getting your cookie:
1. Open open.spotify.com and sign in
2. Open DevTools (F12) > Application > Cookies > https://open.spotify.com
3. Find sp_dc and copy its value
